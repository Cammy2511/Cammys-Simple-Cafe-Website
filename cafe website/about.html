About Us

Cammys Café is a nice place to relax and drink freshly brewed organic coffee and tea, eat delicious pastries, and enjoy the warm atmosphere in the neighborhood.

Our goal is to make a comfortable place for friends and family to get together and make memories that will last a lifetime while enjoying the best café in Kingston.

Cammys Café opened in 2024 and quickly became a favorite place for both locals and tourists. We are proud of our dedication to sustainability. We get our ingredients from local farmers and use packaging that is good for the environment.
